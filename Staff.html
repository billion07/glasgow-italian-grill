Glasgow Italian Grill are a family run restaurant business, established in 2010, in the north of Glasgow. We pride ourselves as one of the finest Italian restaurants in Scotland. Our team have a passion for what we do and take great pride in our classic food and service. Good Italian cuisine uses only the freshest ingredients. That’s why we prepare our sauces and breads daily. In short, we’re passionate about what we do. We love what we do, and we want others to share our enthusiasm. We want you to come and have a great eating out experience.
